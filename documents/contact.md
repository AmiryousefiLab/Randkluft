## Contact & Citation

**Randkluft** is a free research software under active development.

The method and software are currently **under review in *Bioinformatics***.  
Until formal publication, please cite the **Randkluft program link** when using this tool in analyses, presentations, or derived work.

Randkluft is provided *as is* for research and academic use. While we strive for robustness and reproducibility, users are encouraged to visually inspect results and validate gates where appropriate.
